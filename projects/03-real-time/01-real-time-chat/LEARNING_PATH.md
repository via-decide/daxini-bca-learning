## 🎯 Learning Outcomes
✅ **WebSockets** - Understanding persistent, bidirectional communication.
✅ **Event-Driven Architecture** - Emitting and listening to events (e.g., 'message', 'join_room').
✅ **State Management** - Tracking which users are currently online and in which rooms.
