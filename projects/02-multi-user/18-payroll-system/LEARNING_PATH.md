## 🎯 Learning Outcomes
✅ **Financial Math** - Accurately computing Gross to Net via ledger items.
✅ **Immutable Records** - Why a Payslip must be a static snapshot, not a dynamic calculation on the fly.
✅ **Batch Processing** - Running a job on the 25th of every month to generate thousands of records.

## 📋 Project Overview
Build a financial engine that converts annual salaries into monthly paychecks, automatically applying tax brackets and standard deductions.
