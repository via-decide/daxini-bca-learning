## 🎯 Learning Outcomes
✅ **Complex Workflows** - Managing the lifecycle of a contract from Bid to Payment.
✅ **Escrow Logic** - Understanding how platforms hold money (Funded) before giving it to the worker (Released).
✅ **Bid Validation** - Ensuring you can't place a bid on a closed project.

## 📋 Project Overview
Build a gig-economy platform connecting clients who need work done with freelancers who submit competitive proposals and get paid upon milestone completion.
