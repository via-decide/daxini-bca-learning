## 🎯 Learning Outcomes
✅ **Many-to-Many with Attributes** - A tutor teaches Math for $50/hr, but Physics for $80/hr. The rate lives on the Join table.
✅ **Third-Party Integrations** - Conceptually integrating with Zoom or Google Meet APIs to generate meeting links.
✅ **Search Vectors** - Searching for tutors by subject capability.

## 📋 Project Overview
Build a marketplace for education where students search for subject experts, view their schedules, and book paid video sessions.
