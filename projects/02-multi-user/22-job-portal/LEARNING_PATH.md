## 🎯 Learning Outcomes
✅ **Stateful Join Tables** - The `Applications` table connects Users and Jobs, but holds crucial business logic (the status).
✅ **Role-based Dashboards** - The API must serve totally different views for Applicants (My Applications) vs Employers (My Job Postings).
✅ **Pagination & Filtering** - Searching jobs by location, salary, and keyword.

## 📋 Project Overview
Build a two-sided marketplace where employers post job descriptions and job seekers submit their resumes, complete with an employer dashboard to review candidates.
