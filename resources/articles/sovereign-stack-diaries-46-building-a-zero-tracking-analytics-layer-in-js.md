We respect user privacy by avoiding invasive third-party analytics services like Google Analytics. Instead, we built a lightweight, zero-tracking analytics layer that runs entirely on our local edge servers.

Our script logs anonymous site interactions, such as page views and subscription clicks, sending the data to our local database. No cookies are stored, and no IP addresses or personal identifiers are collected.

This privacy-first analytics setup provides us with the necessary metrics to monitor site performance while guaranteeing complete user anonymity, aligning with our zero-cloud, privacy-first philosophy.
