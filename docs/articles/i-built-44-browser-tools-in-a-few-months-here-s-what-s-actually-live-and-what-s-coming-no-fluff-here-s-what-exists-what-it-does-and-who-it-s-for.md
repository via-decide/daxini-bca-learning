This one is different from the rest. It's a full browser game running on Three.js — no download, no app store, just open the link.

You start as a dormant seed. You grow by spending water and nutrients. Pests show up. Stress builds. Push too hard without resting and your crop burns. Hit the thresholds and your tree evolves — from Sprout to Sapling to Young Tree.

Every action is tied to a real career metaphor. Water is consistency. Nutrients are learning. Pests are distractions. Credits are the career capital you've built. The game doesn't tell you what to do — it shows you what happens when you do or don't.

It runs at:

via-decide.github.io/decide.engine-tools/tools/engine/growth-milestone-engine/index.html

Five mini games are queued. All browser-native, all connected to the Orchard state engine:

🐍 Snake — score feeds into your fruit production meter

🃏 Memory Match — card flip game, 16 cards, best time saved

⌨️ Typing Speed — WPM test with orchard-themed sentences

🧩 Resource Puzzle — allocate water/nutrients across three tree slots, maximise yield

📝 Quiz Engine — knowledge check that unlocks orchard progression

Each one connects to the same localStorage state. Your snake high score is fruit. Your typing speed is water consistency. The metaphor holds across the whole system.

Zero frameworks. Every single tool is three files: index.html, tool.js, config.json. They talk to each other through the browser's native CustomEvent API. State lives in localStorage under one key. No React, no bundler, no Node server.
